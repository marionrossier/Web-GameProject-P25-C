Screenshots of the Game "Mouse Rush"

By Valentin, the 03.04.2025

Here are the different stages of the user experience in the game, from the main menu to the death screen.

Main Menu

The game's home screen, where players can choose to play or view instructions.

Instructions

The instructions explain the game rules: guide the red ball through the maze without touching the walls.

Play

Face the mazes in increasingly complicated worlds.

Play - Timer

Track the time for each race live to compare your performance.

Play - Fog

As you progress, the difficulty increases, and you will find yourself in a fog, limiting your view of the game.

Death Screen

The screen displayed when a player touches a wall, losing a life, with options to continue or return to the menu.

Themed maps - Dungeon

A themed map featuring a dungeon environment with dark and mysterious visuals.

Desert

A themed map showcasing a desert environment with sandy textures and warm tones.
